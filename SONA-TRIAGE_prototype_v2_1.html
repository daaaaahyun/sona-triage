About this prototype: This is an interactive concept demonstration built for a humanitarian engineering course project, not a functioning medical device. The computer-vision, reinforcement-learning guidance, and deep-learning diagnosis modules are simulated with rule-based logic and scenario-scripted outputs standing in for models that would be trained on real ultrasound datasets (e.g. FAST-exam corpora). Voice guidance uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis, running entirely client-side — consistent with the project's offline, edge-AI design goal. No real patient data is used or collected.
